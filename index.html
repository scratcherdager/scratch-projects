Hello and welcome!

hello and welcome to the offical scratcherdager-projects website! here we have all projects made by scratcherdager on scratch!

Projects:

1. the cat and the ball

  instructions: W key is up S key is down D key is right and A key is left, you
  have 3 health and if you touch a ball 1 health goes away and the cat says: meow!
  If you press key 1: the cat will also say meow! Space key is to reset the game.
  notes and credits: use this on computer, game controller, or micro:bit

2. mouse vs cats

  instructions: W key is up S key is down D key is right and A key is left, you
  have 3 health and if you touch a cat 1 health goes away and the mouse says: piep
  piep! If you press key 1: the mouse wil also say piep piep! notes and credits:
  use this on computer, game controller, or on micro:bit.

3. the wizard chase

  instructions: W key is up, and S key is down, notes and credits: use this on
  computer and micro:bit only

4. math challenge

  instructions: put the answer in the typebar. you have a time limit and if 5
  answers were wrong then you're game over! notes and credits: use this on
  computer, phone, or tablet.

5. mouse vs stars

  instructions: W key is up. S key is down. Time limit: 60 seconds. If you touch a
  star you will gain a score point. notes and credits: use this game on computer,
  game controller, and micro:bit only!

6. minecraft

  instructions: 1: W-A-S-D = move, jump and swim. 2: click = dig or place blocks.
  3: E = open inventory or block. 4: F = eat food item 5: space = drop/split item
  while crafting 6: O = save current game 7: P = pause current game 8: C =
  commands 9: V = advancements 10: R = sprint 11: to open crafting table, chest or
  furnace, put your mouse pointer over it and press: E notes and credits: use this
  on computer.

7. pencil

    instructions: you just draw anything.
    if you don't like it click the eraser button to erase the part you don't like.
    to draw again just click the pen button to draw even more cool stuff.
    notes and credits: use this on computer, phone, or tablet.

8. memory game

    instructions: press the green flag and press: play,
    then you have to memorize the numbers you get.
    notes and credits: use this on computer, phone, or tablet.
